# Dictation Practice - User Guide

## Overview

The Dictation Practice feature helps users improve their listening and typing skills by listening to translated sentences and typing what they hear.

## How to Access

### From Exercise Card
1. Complete a translation exercise first
2. A purple "Dictation" button will appear on the exercise card
3. Click the button to start dictation practice

### From Exercise Detail Page (Review Mode)
1. Complete a translation exercise
2. Navigate to the exercise in review mode
3. Click "Practice Dictation" button

### Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Enter**: Submit answer or advance to next sentence
- **Space**: Play/pause audio (when not typing)
- **Arrow Keys**: Navigate between sentences
- **R**: Replay audio

### Typing Your Answer

1. Listen to the audio by clicking Play
2. Type what you hear in the text area
3. Click "Check Answer" or press Enter
4. Review your feedback

### Understanding Feedback

#### Accuracy Scores
- **Character Accuracy**: How accurately you typed each character
- **Word Accuracy**: How many words you got correct

#### Mistake Categories
- **Missing Words**: Words you didn't type

- **Misspelled Words**: Words you typed incorrectly

### Passing Criteria

- You need **75% accuracy** to pass a sentence
- Sentences with passing scores are marked as completed
- You can retry sentences as many times as needed

### Session Completion

- Complete all sentences to finish the session
- Your progress is automatically saved
- View your overall accuracy and completion stats
- Earn achievements for your performance

## Progress Tracking

### Achievements

- **First Dictation**: Complete your first dictation practice
- **Dictation Master**: Complete 10 dictations with 90%+ accuracy
- **Perfect Dictation**: Achieve 100% accuracy on any dictation

### Session Persistence

- Your progress is automatically saved as you work
- If you leave and return within 24 hours, you can resume where you left off
- Sessions older than 24 hours are automatically cleared

## Tips for Success

1. **Listen Carefully**: Play the audio multiple times if needed
2. **Adjust Speed**: Use slower speeds (0.5x or 0.75x) when learning
3. **Focus on Accuracy**: Take your time to type correctly
4. **Review Mistakes**: Learn from the feedback to improve
5. **Practice Regularly**: Consistent practice improves listening skills

## Accessibility Features

## Troubleshooting

### Audio Not Playing
- Check if your browser supports text-to-speech
- Ensure your device volume is turned up
- Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge recommended)

### Progress Not Saving
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you're logged in
- Clear browser cache and try again

### Can't Access Dictation
- Make sure you've completed the translation exercise first
- Check that you have a valid translation saved
- Try refreshing the page
